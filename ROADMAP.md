# Overview

Project Lumina documents the reverse engineering of the factory illuminated Volkswagen ID.7 emblem system using official Volkswagen Current Flow Diagrams, OEM parts, physical measurements and real-world testing.

Every discovery is classified as:

- ✅ Confirmed
- 🟡 Working Theory
- 🔬 Measured

🟡 Working Theory – Strong engineering evidence but awaiting verification.

🔬 Measured – Verified directly on the vehicle.
